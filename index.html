What is UNIC?



The main reason why the small and medium scale outlets and shops are unable to leverage the available technology is the lack of technical knowledge. To solve this problem the first thing we are doing is creating another layer of abstraction over the existing layer (which is exploited by coders and developers) for creating apps. This layer will allow each and every shop to create their own private and personalized app-like interface to interact with their customers via simple excel sheets and various other tools which they know to use under 5 minutes. The shopkeepers have to follow a simple set of constraints while filling the data that they require in the spreadsheet. We will generate all the possible designs and containers of a ready to use app for them. They can then simply select and customize the designs that they like. They can change the data and designs whenever they like. This will be similar to having a personal app for the shops and outlets where they can regularly notify the customers about their schemes and products, receive and deliver orders, start their loyalty program or even a platform to present their enterprise and help them provide their customers with better services and better experience. All these will ensure higher customer retention and wider reach. This will also be extremely handy to start-ups as well to increase their reach. Our app will be a social network for all such stores (interfaces). Such a network will also help shops to do focussed hyper local advertisements in the local market. All they have to do is to make customers to subscribe to their network. Customers do not need to download and install different apps for different stores, instead our app can be set to different shop interfaces which is equivalent to having an app of that store. This will act as a modern visiting card for the shops. What customers will get is more choices and discounts as well as ease of purchase. We also plan to add delivery option for the customers after establishment period is over.

Problem we are addressing

Having an online mobile application isnâ€™t just a nice to have option for businesses any more. It is a medium for them to communicate to their customers where they are i.e. via smartphones. Even when customers are expecting powerful mobile experiences from businesses of all sizes, it is very difficult and non-economical for businesses to create one. Small and medium scale outlets and shops are unable to leverage the available technology to stay connected to their customers, showcase their product range or shout out their latest offers or create a brand value. They cannot afford any form of advertisements and as a result they are losing a lot of customers to their competition. Why UNIC?

Although there are a few online solutions for creating drag and drop websites for organisations we believe that having an app is more useful for businesses as it allows businesses to stay continuously in touch with their customers and customers are more likely to open an app again as compared to websites.
